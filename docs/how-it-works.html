How It Works

From kitchen to doorstep, every delivery is sealed, scanned, and verified on the blockchain.

Three simple steps
1

Sealed at the Restaurant

Kitchen crew applies a SealTrust tamper-evident label and scans the QR code. The seal event is recorded on-chain with a timestamp, GPS location, and crew member ID.

2

Scanned by the Driver

The delivery driver scans the same QR at pickup, adding a second checkpoint to the blockchain. Any break in the seal between kitchen and handoff is flagged instantly.

3

Verified by You

When your order arrives, scan the QR code to see the full chain of custody. A green checkmark means your food is untampered. If the seal is broken, you can report it and earn rewards.
