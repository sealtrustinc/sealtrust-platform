How It Works

Four steps to
total food safety.

From kitchen to doorstep, SealTrust creates an unbroken chain of verification that protects everyone in the delivery process.

01

Restaurant Seals Order

Kitchen staff applies a tamper-evident seal with a unique QR code to each order before handoff.

02

Driver Scans at Pickup

Delivery driver scans the seal with the free SealTrust app, cryptographically logging the pickup.

03

Seal Stays Intact

The tamper-evident seal protects the order throughout the entire delivery journey.

04

Customer Verifies

Customer scans or checks the seal on arrival. Intact seal means untouched food. Peace of mind, delivered.
